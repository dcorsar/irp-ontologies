The IRP Transport ontology

The IRP Transport ontology (Ontology) VC Definition In use

The IRP Transport Ontology is a vocabulary for describing aspects of public transport services and maps of their routes, developed during the Informed Rural Passenger project at the dot.rural Digital Economy Hub, University of Aberdeen, UK supported by the award made by the RCUK Digital Economy programme; award reference: EP/G066051/1.
