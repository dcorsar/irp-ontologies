The IRP Sensors ontology

The IRP Sensors ontology (Ontology) VC Definition In use

The IRP Sensors Ontology is a vocabulary for describing mobile devices (Apple mobile phones and Android mobile phones) and their sensors. The ontology was developed during the Informed Rural Passenger project at the dot.rural Digital Economy Hub, University of Aberdeen, UK uspported by the award made by the RCUK Digital Economy programme; award reference: EP/G066051/1. The ontology is extends the Semantic Sensor Network ontology to support integration of sensor data from mobile devices with other sensor data. Mobile devices are described as platforms on which a number of sensors (sensing devices) are deployed.
