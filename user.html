The IRP User ontology

The IRP User ontology (Ontology) VC Definition In use

The IRP User Ontology is a vocabulary for describing users of the GetThere software, developed during the Informed Rural Passenger project at the dot.rural Digital Economy Hub, University of Aberdeen, UK uspported by the award made by the RCUK Digital Economy programme; award reference: EP/G066051/1. The ontology is designed to integrate user profiles with journeys they conduct on public transport.
